# Monetization Strategy

## 1. Freemium Subscription (SaaS)
- **Free:** 
  - 3 AI Cards/day (Watermarked)
  - 3-day Meal Plan
  - Standard Support
- **Premium ($9/month or $19 Lifetime):**
  - Unlimited AI Cards (No Watermark)
  - Full 30-Day Meal Plan + Groceries
  - Priority Generation Speed
  - "Verified" Badge on Shared Cards

## 2. Pay-Per-Download (Micro-transactions)
- Single HD Card Download: $0.99
- Premium Gift Guide PDF: $2.99

## 3. Affiliate Income
- Integration in "Gift Ideas" and "Outfit Recommender".
- Commission on every click/purchase.

## 4. Ad Revenue
- Display Ads on free meal plan pages.
- Sponsored card templates.
